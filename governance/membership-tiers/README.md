# Membership Tiers



| Level              | Description                                    | Tools & Equipment | Privileges                       | Responsibilities                           | Values                       | Policies            | Requirement                               | Engagement Requirement    | Consistency Requirement |
| ------------------ | ---------------------------------------------- | ----------------- | -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------- | ------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | ------------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Explorer           | Guests exploring what they can learn and build | Supervised use    | Workshop Tour                    | Respect space and understand basics        | Basic Understanding          | Basic Understanding | Visits workshop                           | Casual visits             | None                    |
| Tinkerer           | Entry-level builder exploring projects         | Supervised use    | Personal projects (1x / 3 weeks) | Contribute to at least 1 community project | Knows & Starts Demonstrating | Learning & Mindful  | Works on community project                | At least 1x / 3 weeks     | One Month               |
| Innovator          | Proficient in at least 1 field                 | Independent use   | Personal projects (1x / week)    | Contributes to 1 community project         | Knows by Heart               | Follows & Guides    | Active contributor                        | At least 1x / week        | One Month               |
| Trusted Innovator  | Consistent contributor and mentor              | Can teach others  | Personal projects (2x / week)    | Contributes to 2 community projects        | Demonstrates & Mentors       | Enforce & Recommend | Consistent contributor                    | At least 2x / week        | One Month               |
| Keystone Innovator | Highest trusted contributor                    | Full trust use    | Personal projects Anytime        | Guide members & lead projects              | Advocate by Heart            | Enforce & Improve   | High impact contributions, Board-voted    | Almost daily              | 3 Months                |
