# Workshop Facilitator

### Purpose

This policy outlines the responsibilities and expectations of members who are entrusted with a key to the Innovation Labs' Workshop. It ensures that the space is used responsibly and securely.

#### Scope

This policy applies to all members who are issued a key granting access to the workshop.

#### 1. Eligibility

* Only appointed members are eligible for key access.
* Must be 18 years old and above
* Members must undergo orientation and understand the workshop’s safety and operational procedures.
* Members must fully understand the risks and responsibilities of being a key holder.
* Must fully understand the Values of the Community.

#### 2. Key Use Guidelines

* Keys are non-transferable and must not be duplicated without prior approval.
* You must not lend your key or allow others to use it, unless they are part of the Official Workshop Facilitators Team.
* A minimum of one Workshop Facilitator (key holders) must be present to keep the workshop open, an active Workshop Facilitator cannot leave the workshop while it is open. Even for a few minutes.
* The workshop must not be left open unattended.
* The space is for official Innovation Labs activities only.
* Members must not have scheduled classes during their time at the workshop.

### 3. Access and Limitations

* The workshop may only be opened from 7am to 8:30pm.
* 1 innovator should be assigned per non-member visiting the workshop.
* A maximum of 5 non-members can be at the workshop at any given time.
* The Workshop Facilitator is responsible to say “No” if there are more than 15 People in the workshop, prioritizing members with valid reasons to stay.
* Any deviation from these guidelines must obtain prior approval.

### 4. Responsibilities

* Workshop Facilitator understands that they are personally responsible for the safety of all the people in the workshop during their session.
* Make sure that all equipments and tools are used properly and nothing gets lost or misplaced.
* Keep the workshop clean and orderly.
  * Garbage bins must be disposed immediately when full, and bags directly disposed if taken out.
* Power down all equipment not in use.
* Promptly report any damage, safety concerns, or unauthorized access.
* Communicate to the Workshop Facilitators Team when opening and closing the workshop.

### 5. Safety and Security

* Store keys securely; do not leave them in public or shared locations.
* Ensure all doors and access points are locked when leaving.
* Lock the door when alone.
* Lock the door when no-one is at the main room.

### 6. Opening Checklist

* Assess if activity is for Innovation Labs project or community.
* Assess if majority of the people in the workshop are members.
* Assess that you as the Workshop Facilitator can be at the workshop for the entire duration of the session.
* Wear the “Workshop Facilitator” tag

### 7. Closing Checklist

* All equipments and tools are back into their original locations.
* All equipments are powered down properly.
* All garbage are properly disposed
* All tables wiped
* All doors are locked
* All blinds are closed
* All Air conditioners are closed
* All water faucets are closed
* Dispose all trash and place new trash bags.
* Main circuit breaker is turned off
* 3D Printer UPS is turned off
  * not pulled out from the socket, there’s a button to close it inside the cabinet
* Main door is locked.
* Stash the “Workshop Facilitator” tag on its official location
* Report to the Workshop Facilitator Team that the workshop closing checklist is completed.

### 8. Transferring the “Workshop Facilitator” role

* If you need to go out and another key holder is at the workshop. You should officially transfer the Workshop Facilitator role.
* The incoming Facilitator consciously and actively accepts.
* The Workshop Facilitator Tag is physically handed over and worn by the incoming Workshop Facilitator.

### 8. Violations and Revocation

* Breaching any part of this policy may result in immediate revocation of key access.
* Repeat offenses may lead to removal from the Workshop Facilitator privilege.

All Workshop Facilitators must submit this form to attest their understanding and commitment.
