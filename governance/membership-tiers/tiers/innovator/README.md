# Innovator

> Every innovation in history started with someone unknown who simply decided to build.
>
> \~ The Dream Builder

Explorers are guests who want to learn about The Community Workshop, and explore what they can build, contribute and learn.

## Qualifications

Curious, wants to learn or build something.

## Privileges

<i class="fa-eyes">:eyes:</i> Tour around the workshop, projects and members.&#x20;

<i class="fa-person-walking">:person-walking:</i> Purposeful stay at the workshop.

<i class="fa-handshake">:handshake:</i> Meet community members to discuss ideas.

## Responsibilities

* [ ] Respect space and equipments.&#x20;
* [ ] Understand what the community is about.&#x20;
* [ ] Understand and follow the [welcome-to-the-workshop.md](../explorer/welcome-to-the-workshop.md "mention")

## Equipment and Facility Use&#x20;

* [ ] Must be accompanied by an <mark style="color:$danger;">**Innovator**</mark> for any equipment use.

## Restrictions

<i class="fa-triangle-exclamation">:triangle-exclamation:</i> Prolonged stay without intent to understand the community or build projects.

<i class="fa-triangle-exclamation">:triangle-exclamation:</i> Intrusive behaviors that could make the other members feel uncomfortable or unsafe.
