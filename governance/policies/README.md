# Policies

This space houses all community policies, designed to provide structure and guidance for the organization.

#### Policies are double-edged:

1. They create clarity, alignment, and sustainability.
2. Over time, they can become outdated or unnecessary—slowing down decisions, limiting action, and leading to a bad culture.

{% hint style="danger" %}
No policies should be followed blindly. Every policy should be understood, challenged, and improved when needed. If something doesn’t make sense, question it.
{% endhint %}

Everyone is empowered—and expected—to think critically and contribute to refining how we operate.

When in doubt, use your best judgment and return to our values.
