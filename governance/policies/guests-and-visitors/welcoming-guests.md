# Welcoming Guests

This policy complements:

* [Welcome to The Workshop](../../membership-tiers/tiers/explorer/welcome-to-the-workshop.md)
* [Handling School Project Requests](handling-school-project-requests.md)

The goal is to make every guest feel welcomed, understood, and connected to the community while staying aligned with The Workshop mission.

## Welcoming flow

Use this flow every time a new guest visits:

### 1) Introduce yourself and what you do at the workshop

* Share your name and role in the community.
* Briefly explain what you are currently building, learning, or helping with.
* Set a welcoming tone and let them know they can ask questions.

Example:

> Hi! I am \<name>. I usually help with \<electronics/robotics/software/design> and I am currently working on \<project>. Welcome to The Workshop!

### 2) Facilitate questions about the guest and what they are looking to do

Ask simple questions to understand intent, goals, and how to best help:

* What brought you to The Workshop today?
* What do you want to build or learn?
* Is this for personal exploration, school, or work?
* What have you tried so far?

If the request is school-related, apply the guidance from [Handling School Project Requests](handling-school-project-requests.md):

* Support guests who want to learn and actively build.
* Do not do the project for them.
* Redirect respectfully when requests do not align with the mission.

### 3) Introduce the guest to all members currently at the workshop

* Give a short introduction of the guest to members present.
* Share what the guest is interested in building/learning.
* Connect them to members or projects that match their interest.

This helps guests feel included and quickly find people who can guide them.

### 4) Show the QR for the "Welcome to The Workshop" guide

* Show the QR code that points to [Welcome to The Workshop](../../membership-tiers/tiers/explorer/welcome-to-the-workshop.md).
* Ask the guest to open it before they leave and save it for reference.
* Highlight key sections: what The Workshop is, basic policies, and how to join the community channels.

## Minimum standard

A guest is considered properly welcomed when all four actions are completed:

* [ ] Host introduced self and role
* [ ] Host asked discovery questions about guest intent and goals
* [ ] Guest was introduced to members present
* [ ] Guest was shown the "Welcome to The Workshop" QR guide
